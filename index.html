Who Are We?

The world’s energy needs and the means to supply them are constantly changing. FEPL was established in 1999 to bring innovative new technologies to the petrochemical sector in India. Led by a team of experts with over 100 years of combined experience, FEPL was the first company to provide oil mist lubrication systems to the Indian market.

This was done in collaboration with leading manufacturers in India and the United States. Since then, the company has installed oil mist systems in over 36% of the refineries in India, including HPCL – Mumbai, IOCL – Bongaigaon Refinery, and IOCL - Barauni Refinery. FEPL was also the first to introduce oil purification systems that use the method of air/gas stripping. In a number of applications, this method provides a more reliable and cost-effective means of removing water and particulates from oils. FEPL continues to innovate, expanding beyond the petrochemical sector and applying its expertise in oil mist and oil purification systems to other companies in heavy industry. It has also introduced new products designed to meet the stringent conditions of the refinery environment, including CCTV and solar systems. .
